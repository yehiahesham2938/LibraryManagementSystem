Welcome to our Library Management System, a user-friendly platform designed to streamline book borrowing and returns.

Users can explore a curated collection of books, all carefully added and managed by the admin. Borrowing is simple: select from the available in-stock books, ensuring a seamless experience. Once borrowed, users can view their current loans in a dedicated section.

Returning a book is just a click away, automatically updating the system to increase stock and make it available for others. With a focus on efficiency and convenience, our system ensures that book lovers can easily enjoy and manage their favorite reads.
